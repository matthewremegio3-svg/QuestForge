Forged to Empower the Entire Realm

QuestForge unites teachers and students, turning every challenge into a shared victory.

For Teachers
  • Create quests with custom difficulty levels and XP rewards.
  • Award special badges and titles for exceptional student performance.
  • Track class-wide progress and identify students who need support.
  • Review submissions and reward consistent effort.
For Students
  • Turn assignments into missions and earn XP.
  • Watch your character grow as you master subjects.
  • Unlock achievements for speed, consistency, and effort.
  • Compete on the leaderboard and build your rank.
  • Track all your progress through a personal dashboard.

Forged with Purpose

Our Vision
  • Become a leading platform for gamified learning experiences.

  • Build a future where education feels like an adventure.
